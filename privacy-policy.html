Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ihor Urbanskyi ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the Padel Battle mobile application (the "App").

The App is a padel tournament organiser and player rating tracker for local groups. It does not require users to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal contact information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, or other personal contact information directly from users.

Player names and match data entered in the App are stored locally on the user's device and are not sent to our servers.

However, we use third-party services to help us understand app usage and improve app stability.

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the App is used. This may include technical and usage information such as app version, device type, operating system version, screen views, session information, and interaction events.

This data helps us improve the App, fix problems, and understand which features are used most often. We do not use this data to directly identify individual users.

Crash Reporting Data

We use Firebase Crashlytics to receive crash reports when the App crashes or encounters technical issues. Crash reports may include technical information such as device model, operating system version, app version, crash logs, stack traces, and relevant application state at the time of the crash.

This information is used only to diagnose and fix bugs and improve app stability.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services (Android version only)

3. How We Use Information

We use analytics and crash reporting data to:

  • understand how users interact with the App;
  • improve gameplay and user experience;
  • detect and fix crashes, bugs, and performance issues;
  • maintain and improve the App.

We do not sell user data.

4. Local Data

The App stores player names, match results, tournament data, and app settings locally on the user's device. This local data is not sent to our servers.

Users can remove all local data by deleting the App or clearing the App's data where supported by the operating system.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Analytics and crash reporting data are retained by Google/Firebase according to their data retention settings and policies.

If you would like to request deletion of data associated with your use of the App, you can contact us at the email address below. Please note that because the App does not require user accounts, we may not always be able to identify data related to a specific user or device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed specifically to children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete such information.

7. Permissions

The App may use internet access to send analytics and crash reports. The App may also use device vibration or haptic feedback for interaction events where supported by the operating system.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
