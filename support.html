Reporting a Problem

To help us resolve issues faster, please include the following when contacting us:

  • Device model (e.g. iPhone 15 Pro, Samsung Galaxy S24)
  • iOS or Android version
  • App version (found in Settings inside the App)
  • A short description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem, if possible
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if helpful

Common Questions

Do I need an account to use the App?

No. The App does not require login or account registration. Everything works offline and locally on your device.

Are player names or match data sent to your servers?

No. All player names, tournament data, and match results are stored locally on your device and are never sent to our servers.

How are player ratings calculated?

Ratings are updated after each tournament based on your finishing position. The better you place, the more rating points you gain.

Does the App have in-app purchases?

The current version does not include in-app purchases.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored locally on your device. You can remove it by clearing the App's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the App.

Is the App available on both Android and iOS?

Yes. Padel Battle is available for both Android and iOS.
